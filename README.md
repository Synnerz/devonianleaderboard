# devonianleaderboard
The official devonian leaderboard for Hypixel Skyblock

# Purpose
For a long time, the top dungeon records haven't been a fair fight. Most of the leaderboard was just a list of who had the best cheats or macros, which killed the drive to actually get good at the game. The Devonian Leaderboard is here to fix that.

We’re building a space where your time actually reflects your skill. We want to see what players can do on a level playing field, where coordination and practice are the only things that matter. It is definitely harder to play without the shortcuts everyone else uses, and you will probably fail more runs because of it. But in this scene, a legit time is worth more than a thousand cheated ones.

The leaderboard is open for anyone ready to put in the work. Just remember that a smooth sea never made a skilled sailor and good luck on the grind.

# Rules
Your run's category depends on your team members, if most of them are considered "Hypixel Legit" but one is not, then your run will be set to the "Accepted Legit" category.

<details>
    <summary>
        <strong>
            Hypixel Legit (expand)
        </strong>
    </summary>

Modifications in this category are limited to those explicitly allowed by Hypixel or those not properly defined as providing an unfair advantage.
> Note: **most** features in **Devonian** are allowed in this section with some exceptions.

Examples of **Allowed** modifications:
* **Solvers** - Visual highlights for correct answers
* **Input Blocking** - Features that prevent incorrect inputs like "Simon Says block wrong clicks"
* **UI** - Custom terminal GUIs and GUIs rescaling
* **"Macros"** - Chat Macros (1 message -> 1 command/message send action), Keybind Macros (sending command whenever key press), Run/Party Status Messages (300 score message)
* **Run Information** - On Screen Dungeon Map showing only rooms which are visible in the Dungeon Map Item (9th slot item)

Examples of **Disallowed** modifications:
* **Slot binding**
* **Leap Menu Keybinds**
* **Wardrobe Keybinds**
* **Pet Keybinds**
* **Highlighting entities through walls (ESP)**

There also exists a broader "rule" for specific actions called "The 1:1 Rule":
* Any physical input **MUST** result in exactly one (1) output. Each output **MUST** have a maximum of one (1) physical input that triggers it.
</details>

<details>
    <summary>
        <strong>
            Accepted Legit (expand)
        </strong>
    </summary>

Modifications in this category are Quality of Life (QOL) features accepted by the community, due to them fixing mechanical inconsistencies or high latency issues.
> Note: **most** features in **Devonian** and **DevonianSolo** are allowed in this section with some exceptions.

**Allowed** Modifications:
* All features listed in **Hypixel Legit**
* **Latency Fixes** - 50 Ping Dungeon Breaker and visual zero-ping Teleport/Etherwarp
* **Movement** - No Rotate
* **Keybinds** - Slot binding, Wardrobe, Pet, and Leap menu keybinds.
</details>

## **Disallowed** actions
The following actions will result in an immediate disqualification and a potential ban:
* **Automation** - ANY form of automated gameplay, macros or botting
* **Unlisted Features** - The use of any modification not explicitly defined in the categories above.

> If you are unsure about the legitimacy of a specific feature, please open a ticket in (Synnerz) Devonian or M7Guides Discord Server.

# Submission Process
<details>
    <summary>
        <strong>

        </strong>
    </summary>

1. **Record POVs** - Obtain footage from all five (5) party members starting before the run begins
    * **Requirements:** Minimum 1080p resolution and 30 FPS
2. **Upload** - Upload all recordings to YouTube (Unlisted or Public)
3. **Open Ticket** - Join the **(Synnerz) Devonian Discord** or **M7Guides Discord** and open a submission ticket
4. **Submit Data** - Paste the following information into the ticket along with your category:

```
Final Time: 
Floor: 
Legitimacy Category: 
POV 1: [Link]
POV 2: [Link]
POV 3: [Link]
POV 4: [Link]
POV 5: [Link]
```
</details>
